Getting There

Travelling by Car

There is a small carpark at the Maynard. When you arrive, please register your car with reception. There is street parking available nearby if the carpark is full.

Carpark Entrance (What3Words): pumpkin.thus.angry

Travelling by Train

Grindleford Station is the nearest train station and is a 7 minute walk away from the Maynard. Please check the timings of the last train to Sheffield or Manchester before the wedding.

Visit Grindleford Station

Travelling by Bus

There is the 65 bus that runs every 2 hours past the Maynard. Click here for the bus timetable.

Travelling by Taxi
