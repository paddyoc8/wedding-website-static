RSVP

Please make sure that you RSVP by Saturday, 31st May so that we can give final numbers and dietary requirements to the venue.

Getting there

The Maynard, Main Rd, Grindleford, Hope Valley S32 2HE

01433424110

enjoymore@the-maynard.com

The ceremony and the reception are all taking place in the same room. While the room is being changed around, we will head to the outdoor patio or the downstairs bar.

On-site parking is available at The Maynard. Please remember to register your vehicle registration at the reception desk on arrival. If the car park is full, street parking is available nearby.

Train - Grindleford Station is a 5 to 10 minute walk away from The Maynard. There are trains from Sheffield and Manchester that go to this station. Please check when the last train is before travelling.

Bus - There is the 65 bus that runs every 2 hours past The Maynard. Click here for the bus timetable.

Taxis - Please make sure you book ahead as companies such as Uber or Bolt do not operate in the area. Find the contact details of local taxi companies provided by The Maynard below.

Accomodation

There are a few still rooms available for the Friday evening at a rate of £190 per night including breakfast. Please get in contact with us if you are interested in reserving a room. We will be handing in the room reservations soon so please do not wait to get in touch.

You are able to check in to your room from 3 pm. You may wish to check in to your room during the drinks reception (2 pm to 3:45 pm), or you may wish to wait until after the wedding breakfast while the room is being turned around for the reception (6:30 pm to 7:15 pm).

If you do not want to leave your luggage in the car, The Maynard are able to store a small number of bags in their office, however space is limited.

Guest information

We’d love to celebrate with everyone, but we are only able to accommodate those named on the wedding invitation. We appreciate your understanding.

Classic wedding cocktail attire—suits and dresses—are perfect! Feel free to wear summery colours if you’d like, but most importantly, wear whatever makes you comfortable.

The wedding will be indoors. If the weather is nice, we should hopefully be outside on the patio for drinks so bring your sunglasses just in case! All outdoor areas are paved so any type of shoe should be fine.

Please take lots of photos and videos while at the wedding! There will be a QR code to scan for you to send photos and videos to us so that we can turn them into a video keepsake after the wedding.

Arrival

If you have parked in the car park, please head to the reception to register your car registration. You may then head to the main bar to wait before taking your seats for the ceremony.

The bar at The Maynard will be open from 12:30 pm if you wish to get there early. Please ensure you are in the main bar by 1:15 pm at the latest in order to take your seats for the ceremony. Anyone who arrives after the doors have closed for the ceremony will have to wait until after the ceremony ends before joining the wedding.

Please take a look at the homepage or our schedule page for timings.

Food & drink

After the ceremony, there will be sparkling wine and canapes in the garden (weather permitting) from 2:00 pm. The wedding breakfast will begin at 4:00 pm with wine provided on the tables and a toast drink for speeches. Evening food will come out at 8:30 pm.

There are bars in the garden, in the ceremony room and in the main bar if you wish to buy drinks or bar snacks.

Yes! Please include any dietary requirements in your RSVP. Some meals can be adapted for different dietary requirements, otherwise alternative meals will be provided. If you have any questions, please let us know and we can ask the venue.

If you would like non-alcoholic options provided throughout the wedding, please indicate so on your RSVP.

There is a children’s menu available which you can view on the RSVP page. All children aged 12 and under will receive the children’s menu. Children aged 13 to 17 will have the option to choose from either the standard or children’s menu.

End of the Night

At 8:30 pm, bacon butties and mushroom melts (and alternatives based on dietary requirements) will be provided.

The patio will be open until 9:00 pm. After that, you may head downstairs to get some fresh air.

Last orders at the bar are at 11:30 pm and the reception will end at midnight.

parkrun
