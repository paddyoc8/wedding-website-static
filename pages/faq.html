Transportation & arrival

Train - Grindleford Station is a 5 to 10 minute walk away from the Maynard. There are trains from Sheffield and Manchester that go to this station. Please check when the last train is before traveling.

Driving - There is a small carpark at the Maynard and there is nearby street parking. Please remember to register your vehicle registration at the reception desk if you park at the hotel.

Taxis - Find the contact details of local taxi companies below. Please make sure you book ahead as companies such as Uber or Bolt do not operate in the area.

Bus - There is the 65 bus that runs every 2 hours past the Maynard. Click here for the bus timetable.

The bar at the Maynard will be open from 12:30pm if you wish to get there early. You will be invited into the ceremony room at 13:15pm to take your seats. As the ceremony begins at 13:30pm, anyone who arrives after that time will have to wait until after the ceremony ends to join the wedding.

Last orders at the bar are at 11:30pm and the reception will end at midnight.
