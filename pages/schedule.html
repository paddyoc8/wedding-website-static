Schedule of the day

We are so excited to celebrate with all of you. Here we have set out the plan for the day:

FRIDAY 1ST AUGUST

CEREMONY

1:30pm - 2:00pm

Guests are able to arrive at the Maynard from 12:30pm and may head the main bar on arrival. Guests will be called to the Chatsworth Suite from 1:15pm, ready for the ceremony to begin at 1:30pm.

DRINKS RECEPTION

2:00pm - 4:00pm

A welcome drink and canapes will be served in the garden or main bar (weather dependent!) after the service. If you are staying in the hotel, you will be able to check into your room during this time.

WEDDING BREAKFAST

4:00pm - 6:00pm

Eat. Drink. While the Chatsworth Suite is being turned around for the reception, we will go to the main bar or to the patio.

Cake cutting and dancing. Evening food of bacon butties and mushroom melts will be served at 8:30pm. Last orders are at 11:30pm and carriages are at 12:00am.

PARKRUN

9am

We plan to do the Monsal Trail parkrun the morning after the wedding. Anyone is welcome to join us or simply meet us for a coffee in the cafe before we head off on our honeymoon!
