Wedding Schedule

We are so excited to celebrate with all of you. Here we have set out the plan for the day:

12:30 - Arrival

The bar at The Maynard is open from 12:30 for guests.

13:30 - Ceremony

The ceremony will start at 13:30. Doors to the Chatsworth Suite will be closed at 13:25 and anyone not in the room will not be permitted entry.

14:00 - Drinks Reception

Drinks and canapes will be served in the garden. We have a wet weather plan given it is the Peak District!

16:00 - Wedding Breakfast

A three course wedding breakfast will be served in the Chatsworth Suite. Please submit any dietary options via the RSVP forms.

18:00 - Speeches

Speeches from the couple, best man and really anyone else who wants to say something...

19:30 - Reception

The party can finally get started in the Chatsworth Suite. There will be some evening food served at 21:00.

00:00 - Carriages

We've had enough of you at this point.
